Eye Clinic

Caridad Center provides services to uninsured children and adults who reside in Palm Beach County, and live at or below the 200% federal poverty level. Proof of income, a photo ID, proof that you live in Palm Beach County, and a birth certificate for children 18 yrs. and younger is required. New patients must complete the eligibility process before scheduling an appointment with a Clinic
